Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Rubesky Barbecue LLC, doing business as Dickey's Barbecue Pit – Puyallup ("we," "us," or "our"), operates an SMS text-message service to send operational messages to our employees. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights with respect to that information. For opt-out instructions, message frequency, and carrier information, please see our SMS Terms & Conditions.

1. Information We Collect

Through our SMS messaging service, we collect only the information you provided during onboarding:

  • Your name
  • Your mobile phone number

We do not collect any additional personal information through the SMS service itself — including but not limited to location data, device identifiers, browsing history, purchase history, or any other data not explicitly listed above.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your name and phone number solely to:

  • Send you operational text messages you have opted in to receive.
  • Address you personally in those messages.
  • Respond to any replies or requests you send us via SMS.

We will never sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. How We Store Your Information

Your name and phone number are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized personnel operating our SMS service. We retain this information only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

4. Children's Privacy

Our SMS service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

5. Questions

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your information, email us at bbq@rubesky.com.
